# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant version 2.1 by reference:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, level of
experience, or perspective. Contributors are expected to communicate
constructively, disagree professionally, and focus criticism on ideas and code
rather than people.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to Tong Liu at <tong@tsugicinema.com>. Maintainers will review reports
and respond in a manner consistent with the Contributor Covenant enforcement
guidelines.
